What it means to use GitHub

2019-06-14 12:30:45

GitHub has been especially helpful during our journey into coding. Currently we had a huge list of repositories and the ability to push, pull and collaborate on projects. I (Victor) have increasingly enjoyed my experience with GitHub and it's a tool to be reckoned with in the job field. Although Jennifer and I are still new at GitHub, we hope that we will continue to grow in the field and in our knowledge of GitHub.
